About droidconIN

droidconIN's goal is to expose developers to the latest technological developments in the Android ecosystem and how they can apply these technological advances when they develop apps for Android.

Who’s coming?

droidconIN is an annual gathering of mobile developers who develop for various platforms including Android.

droidconIN happens on 10-11 November this year. The conference will bring together 500-600 Android and cross-platform developers with a minimum of two years' experience building mobile apps.

Android developers, cross-platform developers, startup founders and CTOs, engineers and senior engineers from mid-size to large companies such as Flipkart, Myntra, BookMyShow, etc and UI designers.

The conference will cover talks on core Android technology – toolchains, libraries, SDKs/APIs. The conference also features talks on how large organizations – such as Myntra, BookMyShow, Dropbox – manage their Android team and organize code / make tech choices to manage scalability and expedite releases. There will also be app demos and lightning talks from the community at the conference, coupled with workshops and Birds of Feather (BoF) sessions.

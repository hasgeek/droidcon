About droidconIN

droidconIN is an annual gathering of mobile developers who build for various mobile platforms, primarily Android. The conference's goal is to expose developers to the latest technological advances in the mobile ecosystem and the means to apply them when they build apps for mobile. Most of the speakers and attendees tend to be Android developers.

Who’s coming?

droidconIN happens on 10-11 November this year. The conference will bring together 500-600 Android and cross-platform developers with a minimum of two years' experience building mobile apps.

Android developers, cross-platform developers, startup founders and CTOs, engineers and senior engineers from mid-size to large companies like Flipkart, Myntra, BookMyShow, etc and UI designers are expected to attend. droidconIN will also have companies operating in the B2B space, building apps for businesses and enterprises. We expect to see OEMs, startups running apps, developers who have their apps in the Playstore and App Store, e-commerce, fintech, data analytics, web and mobile development firms/studios. The speakers will also belong to this pool.

The conference will cover talks on core Android technology – toolchains, libraries, SDKs/APIs. The conference also features talks on how large organizations – such as Myntra, BookMyShow, Dropbox – manage their mobile team, organize code, and make tech choices to manage scalability and expedite releases. There will also be app demos and lightning talks from the community at the conference, coupled with workshops and Birds of Feather (BoF) group discussion sessions.
